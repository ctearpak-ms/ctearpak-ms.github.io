Try Out Azure OpenAI Studio and Chat-GPT

Created by Rick Kemery

Demo deployed Azure OpenAI models in Azure OpenAI studio - how to.



1. Click "Create"

2. Create a name for your model.

3. Select the model you'd like to deploy.

4. Click "Model"

5. We'll choose text-davinci-003 for the demo. But you can deploy many others!

6. Click "Save"

7. Click "Succeeded"

8. Click "Go to Azure OpenAI Studio"

9. Select the subscription and resource.

10. We previously deployed a demo resource. Select yours here.

11. Click "Use resource" to start.

12. Click "ChatGPT playground" to start demoing ChatGPT!

13. Click "Create new deployment" to start a new ChatGPT deployment.

14. Select the model name and deployment.

15. Give your deployment a name.

16. Click "Create" to deploy.

17. Quick overview step 1.

18. Quick overview step 2.

19. Quick overview step 3.

20. Setup the prompt with a specific demo assistant.

21. Assistant setup options.

22. After its selected, the session will update.

23. Start chatting with the new assistant. Type the chat here.

24. Click "Deployments" to show current deployments.

25. Click "Models" to show current models.

26. Question we enter is "what is the price of the current xbox?" - an example question for our assistant.

27. ChatGPT answers about the price then we ask "when is the next xbox scheduled to be released?"

28. ChatGPT answers our second question.

29. Let's try out GPT-3. Click GPT-3 on the side bar.

30. We ask, "what is the weather like in south America? [[enter]]"

31. Click "Generate"

32. GPT-3 responds to our question.

33. Let's generate a quiz. Click here.

34. Select "generate a quiz."

35. Click "Generate"

36. Clicking generate multiple times gives us a few iterations.

37. There's plenty more to do in OpenAI Studio. But for now, you've deployed a model, selected an assistant, and asked a few questions.
